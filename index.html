TechTrove

The IT Club is a vibrant and inclusive community dedicated to fostering a love for technology among students at Garden City University. Comprised of enthusiastic individuals from diverse academic backgrounds, we're united by our shared passion for innovation and learning in the ever-evolving field of information technology.

What We Do ?

At the IT Club, we believe in the power of collaboration, exploration, and hands-on learning.Joining the IT Club isn't just about expanding your technical skills it's about immersing yourself in a supportive community of fellow tech enthusiasts, where you'll find endless opportunities to grow, learn, and make lasting connections.

Hackathons & Competitions

Put your skills to the test and unleash your creativity in hackathons and competitions where you'll tackle real-world challenges and showcase your innovative solutions.

Guest Speaker

Gain valuable insights and perspectives from seasoned professionals and thought leaders in the tech industry through our guest speaker series, where you'll discover new career paths and industry trends.

Hands-on Projects

Collaborate with like-minded peers on exciting IT projects, from developing innovative apps and software to exploring the world of data science and beyond.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with industry professionals, alumni, and fellow students passionate about technology, building valuable connections and expanding your network.
